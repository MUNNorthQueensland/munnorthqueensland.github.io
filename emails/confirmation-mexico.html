Hello esteemed MUNNQ 2020 delegate,

We are excited to tell you that at MUNNQ 2020 you will be representing the United Mexican States.

To be successful at the conference, we recommend you research the views that this country holds on the issues that will be debated, as well their geopolitical relationships with other nations. A great place to start is the CIA World Factbook and you can click here to go to the page for Mexico.

We are also providing you with 2 out of the 3 resolutions that will be debated during the conference with a single resolution remaining unseen until then. There is a pre-reading for each resolution topic except the COVID-19 pandemic topic as we thought the context behind that is very easy to find. These pre-readings are packed with targeted context that will help you to understand the fundamentals of each issue. They are a great starting point in becoming more familiar with the details of each issue. The three topics during that will be dealt with during the day are:

  • The question of the management of the COVID-19 Pandemic

  • The question of Climate Refugees | Pre-reading

  • The question of mitigating the impacts of the Yemen Crisis (this is the unseen resolution) | Pre-reading

We will keep you posted in the coming weeks through email as well as more regularly on our Instagram (so go follow us at @mun.nq).

We look forward to seeing you at MUNNQ 2020,
